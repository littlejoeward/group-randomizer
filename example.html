Each Participant (in this example there are three) picks a number between 0 and 1.
The numbers are multiplied by the number of participants and rounded to a whole number (The number will be between 1 and the number of participants)
You then take the sum of the numbers and get the remainder after dividing by the number of participants.
The remainder is the participant that is selected. (remainder 0 is the last participant)
